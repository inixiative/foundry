## inixiative

Based in Uruguay. Building technology for cooperation — identity, governance, and investment infrastructure that lets groups collaborate on complex, long-horizon endeavors with trust.

Foundry builds on inixiative's existing open-source stack: a rules engine for declarative business logic, a multi-agent coordination library purpose-built for AI workflows, and a battle-tested SaaS template with auth, permissions, and multi-tenancy baked in.
